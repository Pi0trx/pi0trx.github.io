The name of the company refers to two main areas of specialisation, which are: public administration reform and human resources management.
The company was established in 2021 and focuses on consultancy services and training courses in different fields of public administration reform. More information in the section: Services
The main partners are:
  • SOFRECO
  • OECD (Program SIGMA)
  • ReSPA (Regional School of Public Administration)
What makes the company special is:
  • specialisation in the services for public administration. Understanding its specifics and procedures, which results in proposing realistic, implementable, tailor-made solutions
  • individualised approach and we care – above all – about the quality.
  • basing provided advice, among others, on rich experience of co-operation with public administration abroad.
